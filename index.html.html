The Legacy

More Than a
Team.

Ferrari is not merely a constructor — it is the soul of Formula 1. Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1947, the Scuderia has competed in every World Championship since the first race in 1950. No team has won more. No team has endured more. No team will ever mean more.

1950
Birth of a Legend
Alberto Ascari claimed Ferrari's first Drivers' Championship, cementing the Scuderia as the team to beat from the very dawn of Formula 1.
1975
The Lauda Era
Niki Lauda delivered a masterclass in machine and mind, securing back-to-back titles with relentless precision and an iron will that defined an era.
2000
The Schumacher Dynasty
Five consecutive Constructors' Championships. Five Drivers' titles for Michael Schumacher. The most dominant period in the sport's history.
2025
The Leclerc–Hamilton Era
Charles Leclerc and Lewis Hamilton. The greatest qualifier of his generation alongside the greatest of all time. Maranello believes — and the world watches.

Ferrari

The DNA

What Drives
Us.

Il Cavallino Rampante

The Prancing Horse is more than a badge. It is a promise — that whoever wears it will not yield, will not settle, and will never stop fighting for the front.

Engineering Obsession

Maranello houses some of the finest engineering minds on earth. Every component, every simulation, every calibration is driven by a singular question: is it fast enough?

The Tifosi

Three hundred thousand fans in the grandstand at Monza. Millions more watching from every continent. The most passionate supporters in sport don't just follow Ferrari — they live it.

Winning Mentality

Second is not a result in Maranello — it is a reason to return to the factory. The relentless pursuit of the top step of the podium defines everything the Scuderia does.

Vertical Integration

Ferrari is unique in Formula 1 — building its own chassis, power unit, and gearbox. Total control. Total accountability. Every success and every lesson is entirely their own.

Living Heritage

From the 125 F1 of 1948 to the SF-25 of today, Ferrari's history is not a museum exhibit — it is the foundation upon which every future championship will be built.
